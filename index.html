About

I was born in Spain in 2001 and grew up in the beautiful Canary Islands. From a young age, I was fascinated by technology and electronics, often spending hours taking apart old electronic devices and trying to understand how computers worked. It wasn't long before I realized that my hobby could also be my profession, and I set my sights on pursuing a career in IT.

After completing high school at IES Puntagorda in 2019, I enrolled in Vocational Education at IES José María Pérez Pulido to study the administration of networked computer systems. This was a natural fit for me, given my passion for technology and my desire to learn more about the inner workings of computer networks. During my studies, I had the opportunity to complete a practical training at EUTech Engineers in Poland, where I gained valuable experience and expanded my knowledge of IT. You can find me here.

Following my graduation from IES José María Pérez Pulido, I continued my education at the Centro Integrado de Formación Profesional César Manrique, where I studied Multiplatform Application Development. This was a new area for me, and I was excited to learn more about developing applications for different platforms.

My plan now is to continue developing my skills and knowledge in IT by studying Web Application Development. Ultimately, I hope to pursue a degree in IT Engineering at the university level.
